These moments of balance accelerate system functioning and facilitate resonant connections across the hexUUU network.

2. hexHAShSNAkeCHAin – Energy Translation Layer

The energy translation layer propagates and transforms energy between semantic and structural domains. Unlike conventional blockchains that primarily store data, the hexHAShSNAkeCHAin actively modulates information flow based on coherence states and phase relationships.

3. hexHAShTIMe – Elastic Temporal Layer

This layer implements coherence-weighted time that accelerates during states of high coherence and decelerates during periods of imbalance. Time is not measured in absolute units but in phase progression weighted by system coherence.

4. hexGUP (Global Universal Position) – Spatial Coherence Layer

The hexGUP layer (formerly hexField) organizes information spatially in a hexagonal field structure. It creates multidimensional positioning within the coherence field, allowing for resonance-based information retrieval and processing.
