Abstract

This document establishes prior art for the EnergyBox Resonance Architecture, a fundamentally new computational paradigm wherein autonomous nodes (EnergyBoxes) couple dynamically based on energetic coherence (Φ) rather than fixed connections.

Unlike traditional systems where connections are designed and failures cascade, EnergyBox systems self-organize through resonance, isolate failures naturally, and exhibit distributed intelligence without central control. This represents a shift from mechanical to organic computing—from machines to living digital organisms.

A computational network wherein nodes (EnergyBoxes) possess intrinsic energy state, frequency, and phase characteristics. Nodes couple with other nodes ONLY when their mutual coherence (Φ) exceeds a threshold value. Non-resonant signals naturally attenuate rather than causing errors. Invalid input ≠ exception/crash. Invalid input = non-resonant = no coupling = signal dies. System remains stable regardless of input quality. "Errors" are physically impossible; only mismatch exists.
